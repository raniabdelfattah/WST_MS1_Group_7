Our Story

Why we love what we do and why we want to share this with you!

We are four friends who bonded over our love for cooking and sharing family recipes. What started as casual cooking sessions turned into this beautiful collection of treasured recipes that have been passed down through our families for generations.

Each recipe here tells a story - from grandmother's secret ingredients to modifications we've made to suit modern kitchens. We believe that food is the best way to preserve memories and create new ones.

Meet the Team

Helena Tantoco

Helena treasures the cooking of her mom and grandmother, considering their dishes restaurant-quality. She spends her free time recreating the recipes from these two amazing women in her household.

Karissa Mae Manicad

As a loving mom of two, Mae enjoys experimenting with different dishes for her family. While she admits her husband is a slightly better cook, she brings her own unique quirks and talents to the kitchen.

Rania Abdelfattah

Growing up in a melting pot of cultures, Rania brings the rich flavors of Arab, Egyptian, and Filipino cuisines to her cooking. She loves preparing meals in her spare time and can whip up amazing egg dishes for others – though she'll pass on eating them herself!

Chadley De Lara

A pescatarian with an adventurous palate, Chadley is game to try almost anything – even turtle dishes! She's also a humble cook in her own right, with secret recipes she's perfected over the years.
